Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Pikake Floral Studio.

We recommend booking wedding florals at least 3–6 months in advance, especially for peak season (May–September). Popular dates fill up quickly, and we want to ensure we have the specific blooms you love. For smaller orders like leis and custom arrangements, one week's notice is usually sufficient.
Yes! We deliver Monday through Saturday across Kailua, Kaneohe, and surrounding Windward Oahu communities. Delivery starts at $10 and increases based on distance. For wedding and event deliveries with setup, there is a separate delivery and installation fee — we'll quote this in your consultation.
For simple leis like plumeria or mixed tropical, we often can accommodate same-day orders (call before noon). Pikake leis require at least 24 hours' notice as they are labor-intensive and we need to ensure fresh blossom availability. For graduation and large lei orders, one week's notice is preferred.
Tropical flowers are seasonal and availability can vary. If a specific bloom isn't available for your date, we'll always consult with you before making any substitutions. We only substitute with flowers of equal or greater quality and never change a design without your approval. We source from local Oahu farms so we often have advance notice of availability.
Yes, we offer a selection of vases, pedestals, and decorative containers for rent. Our rental inventory includes a variety of styles from modern glass cylinders to rustic ceramic bowls to rattan and woven accents that suit Hawaii's aesthetic beautifully. Rental fees and deposit policies are discussed during your consultation.
Our wedding packages vary widely depending on the scope of the event. Intimate beach weddings with a bridal bouquet, two boutonnieres, and a ceremony arch might start at $800–$1,200. Full reception florals with multiple centerpieces, ceremony setup, and bridal party flowers typically range from $2,500–$6,000+. We offer free consultations where we build a detailed quote based on your specific needs and budget.
We source as much as possible from local Oahu and neighbor island farms. Tropical blooms like anthuriums, heliconia, ginger, and orchids are often locally grown. Some specialty items like roses and lilies may be sourced from the mainland or other Pacific locations when not available locally. We always tell you where your blooms are coming from.
Yes! Our Weekly Island Bloom subscription delivers a fresh seasonal arrangement to your home or office every week. Subscriptions start at $55/week plus a $10 delivery fee. You can pause or cancel anytime with one week's notice. Subscribe for the whole year and receive 10% off the regular price.
Absolutely! Luau florals are a specialty — we create traditional and contemporary Hawaiian event décor including ti leaf table runners, anthurium centerpieces, entrance arches, and lei stations where guests can make their own leis. Contact us for a custom quote for your event.
