Our Story

Born from a deep love of Hawaii's flowers and a desire to share that beauty with our community.

Our Journey

From a Backyard Garden to Kailua's Beloved Florist

Pikake Floral Studio was born in 2012 when founder Malia Akana began stringing leis from her grandmother's pikake garden in Kailua. What started as a way to honor island tradition grew into a full floral studio when neighbors and friends began requesting her beautiful arrangements for weddings and events.

Today, Pikake Floral is a family operation — Malia's daughter Noelani manages events, while local farmer partnerships ensure every stem is as fresh as the Hawaii morning air. We source from small Oahu growers whenever possible, reducing our footprint and supporting our community.

Whether you're planning a beach wedding, a graduation celebration, or simply want fresh flowers in your home each week, we pour aloha into every petal.
